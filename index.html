Introduction

  1. Among the several environmental crisis that we are facing , water scarcity is increasing at an alarming rate.
  2. One of the main reasons for this is the uncontrolled consumption.
  3. The Smart Water Quality Check Meter not only checks the levels of water consumption, but also the quality of water thereby helping to improve the quality of life.

Objectives

  1. The objective of our project is to introduce a smart water system which helps in water conservation, saves human effort and also helps in improving its quality.
  2. The quality check parameter can be implemented both in domestic households as well as in industries.
  3. The proposed system mainly focuses on the Indian system.
  4. The system developed is expected to be cost effective and user friendly.
